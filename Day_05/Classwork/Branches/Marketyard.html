A library is an integral part of any educational institution. SunBeam Students have long enjoyed the educational and cultural benefits of our library service. Library resources and usage has grown steadily, and today the library at SunBeam Institute of Information Technology is fully equipped with over 100 titles and periodicals. The library currently offers a collection of hundreds of items, including books, magazines, CDs, and more which are required to fulfill needs of a candidate undergoing a CDAC ACTS IT course. It houses books suggested from C-DAC and many other popular titles which help the students to gain additional knowledge in areas of their interest. The library facility at SunBeam offers increased convenience in skill building, which in turn facilitates increased enthusiastic participation of our students in interactive academic sessions.
